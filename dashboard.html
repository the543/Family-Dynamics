Student Dashboard

Welcome to the Student Dashboard, your personalized hub designed to enhance your connection with family while navigating the unique experience of being a residential student. This page serves as a central point for accessing various features tailored to your needs. Here, you can manage communication tools that facilitate real-time chats with family members, share important events through a collaborative calendar, and maintain personalized timelines to celebrate milestones together. Additionally, the dashboard provides access to resources aimed at improving family dynamics, such as tips for maintaining healthy relationships and mental well-being. With an intuitive layout and user-friendly design, the Student Dashboard empowers you to cultivate and strengthen your family connections, ensuring that you never feel distant from those who matter most.
